About

Top Quality. Speedy Service. Creative Mounts.

Creative taxidermy is in Roy, Washington. Roger and Mark believe in classic implementations and creative artistry and demand precision in each piece of work. Full scale or small game. We deliver with pride and honor our customer. We know the importance of enhancing the lifetime of each trophy and hide. We spoil our customers with passion about hunting and maintaining the highest standards for every kill.
